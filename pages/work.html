Work

An overview of organizations and projects I've been involved with

I'm a generalist - I've done a little bit of a lot of things.

I've worked on backend systems with Python(Flask) and Node(Express.js). I've written Android apps (with new libraries like RxJava). I've worked a bit on frontend JavaScript frameworks like Ractive, Vue.js, and Angular with CSS frameworks such as Bootstrap, Pure CSS, Semantic-UI, Foundation, and Bulma.

I have worked on media - recording talks, interviews, and podcasts - including shoot, editing, and post-processing with tools such as Adobe Audition, Premiere Pro, and After Effects.

I've also worked as freelance graphic designer while in college - designing of logos, social media and newsletter templates, mobile app mockups, etc

As an activist and campaigner on digital rights issues, I've designed and developed campaign websites, worked on design and social media, and written content on technical topics.

I've worked extensively with communities, primarily in tech. This has involved defining and curating several conferences, organizing meetups and workshops, as well as finding new ways to engage with them - via newsletters, Slack communities, and discussions.
I also run ReRoll Board Games - a board game community in Bangalore and organize a monthly puzzle event called Puzzled Pint.

I care about building products that are open, ethical, and meaningful.

If you are looking for someone with a mix of engineering, product, and common sense - Get in touch!
